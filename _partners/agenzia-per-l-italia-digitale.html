The Agency for Digital Italy (Agenzia per l’Italia Digitale) is a Government agency responsible for the implementation of the Italian Digital Agenda under the surveillance of the Presidency of the Council of Ministers. Since June 2012 the Agency has replaced the former e-Government agency (DigitPA), the Department for Digitization and Innovation and the Agency for the Promotion of Technology Innovation. The Agency supports public administrations in their effective use of ICT, improving quality of services and reducing costs.

AgID has broad competences in e-Government, information society and technology innovation, including such areas as new generation networks, security, open standards, digital literacy, open data, digital inclusion, smart communities and digital health and education. The Agency promote e-Government by providing support at the legislative, policy-making and implementation levels and by defining regulations and criteria for the design, implementation and management of public information systems.

It also defines criteria and technical rules on ICT security, interoperability and performance. As the former DigitPA, the new Agency maintains Sistema Pubblico di Connettività (SPC), a general architectural framework to provide central and local administration with shared network and application services, which is consistent with the European Interoperability Strategy and Framework. The Agency has published guide-lines on the adoption of cloud services by public administrations that paved the way to further initiatives in data center consolidation and provision of vertical and horizontal cloud services to the public sector. Within its scope, the Agency is the national reference authority for European and international relations and promotes a wider participation to European programs and initiatives.

AgID operates a Computer Emergency Response Team of Public Administration (CERT-PA),which is responsible for the prevention and management of ICT security incidents within the domain constituted by the Italian Public Administrations through the support and coordination of their cyber security teams.

According to the Italian cyber security rules, the CERT-PA is in charge of the providing the administrations with:

  • Services of analysis, aimed to supporting the definition of the security management process, the development of methodologies, the design of processes and evaluation metrics for public administration cybersecurity
  • Proactive services, for the collection and processing of data relevant for the purpose of cyber security, the issuance of security bulletins and reports, deployment and management of security information bases
  • Reactive services, for the security alarm management and the support of the management and resolution processes of security incidents within the domain of the Italian Public Administration
  • Training and communication, to promote the culture of cyber security, enhancing the awareness and expertise within the Italian public administrations through the sharing of information relating to specific events in progress, new risk scenarios or specific issues of information security.

Roles in the project

  • Contribution on the design, architecture and business models for the project
