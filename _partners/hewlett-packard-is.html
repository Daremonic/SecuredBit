--- title: Hewlett-Packard Limited (Information Security) (HPIS) layout: partner partner: hpis ---

HP Information Security UK / HP Enterprise Security Services provides managed (outsourcing), consulting and advisory security services to many of the largest private and public sector organisations globally. It operates eight security operations centres globally, protecting its clients 24x7. Such managed services offered are remote security monitoring, correlation, alerting, incident management and response, threat intelligence and security device management such as firewalls, VPNs and IDS/IPS.

It also provides its clients with advisory and consulting services in the following areas: Digital Forensics Incident Response, Security Information and Event Management, Threat and Vulnerability Management, Security Strategy and Risk Management, Identity and Access Management, Data Protection and Privacy, Applications Security and Infrastructure and Network Security.

HP Enterprise Security Services maintains strong partnering relationships with the other leading security vendors, therefore delivering best of breed security solutions to its customers.

Roles in the project

  • Delivering managed security services globally to the project
  • Contribution on the requirements for how vNSFs will be architected in order to meet the requirements of Managed Security Service Providers
  • Contribution on the vNSF development by porting existing open source security tools to be utilised as vNSFs that will be integrated in the project
